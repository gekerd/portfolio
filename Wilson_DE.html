Digital Electronics

Project: Project 1.2.7 Bike Lock

Course: Digital Electronics (DE)

Duration: 10/12/2020 - 10/21/2020

Objective: Create a prototype bike lock

Type of Project: Individual Project

This was the first project experienced in DE. For this project, I simulated a working bike lock by representing it using circuits. I began by showing how my circuit would look. I used a program called Multisim to display a working digital simulation of the bike lock. I used this simulation as a basis for a real life circuit that also represented the bike. For this, I used a breadboard to show how the prototype circuit would work. Only one combination of 3 digits (in binary) would unlock the bike lock. For the documentation, click here.
